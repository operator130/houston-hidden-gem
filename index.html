Cute & Cozy


Located in the Peacock and Plaza Court Apartments, The Hidden Gem is a vintage charm in the heart of Downtown Houston. Originally established in the 1920's, the apartment reflects an eclectic style reminiscent of Houston's building boom. Inside, guests will find a newly designed interior with amenities that accomodates today's traveler. Chic, stylish, and refined--Make your Houston getaway a trip to remember at The Hidden Gem!

Conveniently Located


The Hidden Gem is a vintage charm in the heart of Downtown Houston. This special place is close to everything, making it easy to plan your visit. It sits across the street from the Toyota Center, walking distance to the George R. Brown Convention Center and a 5 minute commute to Dynamo & Minute Maid Stadium. Bars & restaurant options are only footsteps away. Whether you're here for leisure or business, everything Houston has to offer is right at your doorstep.
