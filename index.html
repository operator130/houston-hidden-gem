Cute, Cozy, and Conveniently Located


The Hidden Gem is a vintage charm in the heart of Downtown Houston. This special place is close to everything, making it easy to plan your visit. It sits across the street from the Toyota Center, walking distance to the George R. Brown Convention Center and a 5 minute commute to Dynamo & Minute Maid Stadium. Bars & restaurant options are only footsteps away. Make your Houston getaway a trip to remember!
