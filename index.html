Conveniently Located


Located in the Peacock and Plaza Court Apartments, The Hidden Gem is close to everything, making it easy to plan your visit. It sits across the street from the Toyota Center, walking distance to the George R. Brown Convention Center and a 5 minute commute to Dynamo & Minute Maid Stadium. Bars & restaurant options are only footsteps away. Whether you're here for leisure or business, everything Houston has to offer is right at your doorstep.
